Arraial D'juda | Bahia-Brazil

The place that connects you to nature

Reasons:

Carmo's Cottages is a comfortable, beautiful and peaceful place, with a direct connection with nature, at the top of Pitinga Beach with a privileged view. Wake up every day to birdsong, the sound of the sea and a privileged view of the sunset and sunrise.

Our Cottages are located on the seafront with private access to the beach. The main house was decorated in the Balinese theme, with a beautiful garden surrounded by greenery, coconut trees and loose animals such as Peacock, a typical animal from Brazil. Arraial D'ajuda, provides a romantic and pleasant atmosphere with a lot of tranquillity and nature.

Our goal is to offer a connection with nature that is pleasant and invigorating. Our Cottages are all equipped with no need to worry about anything. We offer Cottages for couples and families. We have an area for socializing with a barbecue in front of two pools (one for children and one for an adult) with excellent views of the sea. Come and enjoy Arraial D'Ajuda with us, host in our Cottages
