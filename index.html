Arraial D'juda | Bahia-Brazil

The place that connect you with nature

Reasons:

Carmo's Cottages is a comfortable, beautiful and peaceful place, with direct connection with nature, at the top of pitinga beach with a privileged view. Wake up every day to birdsong, the sound of the sea and a privileged view of the sunset and sunrise.

Our chalets are located on the sea front with private access to the beach. The main house was decorated in the Balinese theme, with a beautiful garden surrounded by greenery, coconut trees and loose animals such as Pavao, a typical animal from Brazil, Arraial D'juda, providing a romantic, pleasant atmosphere with a lot of tranquility and nature.

Our goal is to offer a connection with nature that is pleasant and invigorating. Our chalets are all equipped with no need to worry about anything. We offer chalets for couples and families. We have area for socializing with barbecue in front of the pools (one for children and one for adult) with excellent views of the sea. Come and enjoy Arraial d'juda with us, host in our Cottages.
